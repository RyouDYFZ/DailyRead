Even amid a tumultuous few weeks for tech stocks, SpaceX has taken a particular hit.

After an initial investor frenzy that saw the company valued at more than Amazon and Microsoft, the price of its shares has drifted downward.

XAI is best known for the controversial chatbot Grok, but through that acquisition, SpaceX now leases data centre capacity to other tech companies.

The company's main business is the manufacture and launch of rockets and telecommunications satellites called Starlink.

When Starlink said it was cutting prices in the Memphis, Tennessee area amid local concerns over a massive data centre project, SpaceX shares fell by 8%.

SpaceX is expected to release in August its first public earnings report.

Sosnick added: "The fact that a stock has fallen a couple of dollars below its IPO price in itself is not a tragedy, but SpaceX is heavily watched and has an important role in investor psyche."

SpaceX did not immediately respond to a request for comment.

## 长难句理解

> SpaceX's share price has dropped below its stock market debut just over a month ago, falling sharply from a post-float peak.

**语法要点：**现在分词短语作伴随状语

**分析：**主句为“SpaceX's share price has dropped below its stock market debut just over a month ago”，现在分词短语“falling sharply from a post-float peak”作伴随状语，说明股价下跌的幅度和比较基准。

**翻译：**刚刚过去一个多月，SpaceX的股价就已跌破其股市首秀价格，较上市后的高点大幅下挫。


> If the price holds, or falls further, it will mean that those who purchased stock around the time of its flotation will stand to lose money on their investment.

**语法要点：**第一条件句；定语从句；短语“stand to do”

**分析：**该句为第一条件句，主句“it will mean”后接由that引导的宾语从句，宾语从句中又包含“who purchased stock around the time of its flotation”修饰“those”的定语从句。“stand to lose”表示“有可能损失”。

**翻译：**如果股价维持不变或进一步下跌，这将意味着那些在公司上市前后购买股票的人有可能亏损。


> After an initial investor frenzy that saw the company valued at more than Amazon and Microsoft, the price of its shares has drifted downward.

**语法要点：**时间状语从句；定语从句；短语动词

**分析：**“After an initial investor frenzy”为时间状语，后跟that引导的定语从句修饰“frenzy”，主句为“the price of its shares has drifted downward”。“drifted downward”为短语动词，表示“逐渐下跌”。

**翻译：**在初始的投资者狂热使公司估值超过亚马逊和微软之后，其股价已逐渐下跌。


> When Starlink said it was cutting prices in the Memphis, Tennessee area amid local concerns over a massive data centre project, SpaceX shares fell by 8%.

**语法要点：**时间状语从句；宾语从句；介词短语作状语

**分析：**“When Starlink said...”为时间状语从句，其中“it was cutting prices...”为said的宾语从句；“amid local concerns over a massive data centre project”为介词短语作状语，说明背景。主句为“SpaceX shares fell by 8%”。

**翻译：**当星链表示因当地对一个大型数据中心项目的担忧而在田纳西州孟菲斯地区降价时，SpaceX股价下跌了8%。

## 习语与地道表达

**take a hit** 遭受损失或打击

非正式用语，常用于商务或财经语境，指价值、销量等显著下降。

例句：The company's reputation took a hit after the scandal.


**stand to lose** 可能损失

用于描述潜在的风险或不利结果，后接具体内容，如金钱或机会。

例句：If the deal falls through, we stand to lose millions.


**in itself** 本身；就其本身而言

用于强调某事物独立来看的性质，常与否定或限定词连用，表示不构成重大问题。

例句：The mistake, in itself, is not fatal, but the cover-up could be.
